Our Mission

eNGOy is an initiative of Sheraz Alam, a 15-year-old high school student from Mysuru, India. There are many not-so-well-known NGOs that close due to lack of funds, eNGOy is a non-profit organization that connects donors to such small NGOs all over India. This user-friendly platform does not charge any fee on donations made by individuals to needy NGOs. A thorough screening of NGOs ensure that only the genuine ones are on-boarded. Donations made here are also eligibile for tax exemptions as per the NGOs' certifications. Through this platform, we strive to raise awareness and build trust for the social sector. We, at eNGOy, are committed to our purpose of supporting NGOs that work with a burning desire to make a difference!
